Places must visit in Himachal

Naina Devi

i had visited in june 2016

Shri Naina Devi Ji is one of the most notable places of worship in Himachal Pradesh. Located in Distt Bilaspur, it is one of the 51 Shaktipeeths where limbs of Sati fell on Earth. This holy place witnesses the huge crowd of pilgrims and devotees round the year and especially during Shravan Ashtami and in the Navratras of Chaitra & Ashwin.

Kangra Devi

Vajreshwari Devi Temple is located at Kangra town of Kangra District, Himachal Pradesh. The temple is also known as Kangra Devi Temple. The Vajreshwari temple is one of the 51 Shaktipeeths in India. The temple is dedicated to the Goddess Sati. It is also known as Shree Nagarkot Devi temple. It is believed that original mandir was built by Pandavas. Morgan’s Place, Mcleod Ganj

class="text-muted text-left" >It's a wonderful Place.Most must-visit lists for Mc Leodganj are incomplete without this spot. A short, steep walk off the trek to Dharamkot leads to this buzzing cafe. There’s an indoor seating space for those who’d like, but we recommend sitting in the balcony area. Enjoy comfort foods like pancakes and paninis, lemon iced teas and piping hot teas while you enjoy unobstructed views of the mountains. The wood-fired oven sits at the end of the balcony, where you can watch steaming pizzas dished out with your favourite toppings.

The Johnson’s cafe, Kullu

Some 200 metres from Mall Road in Manali, Johnson’s Café sits pretty as part of The Johnson's Hotel. Crowded forests line the windows on one side here, while the mountains extend far and wide on the other. The wooden seating of the space is accented by vibrant and colourful decor. There’s ample to choose from the menu that features Italian and European food, but the café is popular for its wood-fired pizzas and fresh trout fish. Enjoy your meal to the sound of the river in the day, or head here in the evening for some live singing. Address: Circuit House Road, Siyal, Kullu, Manali, Himachal Pradesh; Instagram; Contact no: 08626814404
